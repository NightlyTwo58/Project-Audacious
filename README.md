# Project-Audacious
Project related to a messaging application.

Our idea is to make a messaging app that emphasizes the unique connection of trust between sets of users, and as such is targeted towards an audience of family and close friends. Our selling point? The ability to read other's direct messages as they type, not after they send. This currently isn't very popular in existing message applications due to implications about user privacy- you wish to think while typing- but with the correct userbase and trust built into the app, this can be a prized feature.
